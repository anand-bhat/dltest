This is my collection of tools and links for the DreamLab app.
DreamLab is an app that runs on smartphones and tables on the Android or iOS platforms. The app uses the processing power of these devices to perform scientific calculations that helps researchers worldwide conduct useful investigations in various areas of medical research, primarily cancer and COVID-19.
Download the DreamLab app to start participating today!

Online tools:

  • Projects - Check the progress of various projects running on DreamLab.
  • Communities - Engage with other DreamLab participants.
  • Articles - Press releases and online articles about DreamLab.
